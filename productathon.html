Productathon'21

We are organising a 48 hours long hackathon

Get Started

PRODUCTATHON, a 48 hour hackathon event, is a software and simulation based design sprint event,

where the enthusiasts across various domains will work in small groups to create a scalable prototype before a deadline as per the problem statement/theme. We aspire to provide you with the best possible experience, opportunities and an avenue to enrich themselves with knowledge about the entrepreneurial world.

  • All teams consists of upto 5 participatns
  • Following a event theme ideas are pitched to solve an existing problem
  • Shortlisted teams go on further to design and develop a working prototype for their solutions and for this they are given a 24 hours time and mentors to guide their ideas with more additional hardware available to realize their products

Winners get awarded from our Dean himself along with many gifts and goodies from our event sponsors and an amazing project to showcase in your resume for years to come
